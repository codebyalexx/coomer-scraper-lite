## Tips for Interpreting the Display

1. **Overall Progress Bar**: Best indicator of total progress
2. **Active Downloads**: Number after `â†“` symbol shows concurrent downloads
3. **File Sizes**: Large files (videos) will show slower progress
4. **Failed Downloads**: Stay red for 3 seconds - watch for patterns
5. **Skipped Files**: High skip count means most files already downloaded
6. **Artist Progress**: Shows how many posts processed for that artist
7. **Post Progress**: Shows how many files processed for that post
